ABOUT US

Kingdom Coffee is a volunteer driven coffee shop designed to foster community. Our goal is to deliver high quality products; while maintaining low cost in order to serve our community with our available resources. We offer a wide variety of espresso creations, teas, cocoas and baked goods for our customer's enjoyment. Whether you join us in our shop or just pop in for a quick to-go we stand ready to serve you. We're all about community!!!

OUR COFFE

Coffee Blends and Single Origins :

ESPRESSO COFFEE :

Espresso coffee is a type of coffee made by adding hot water to finely ground coffee and
is characterized by its strong and concentrated taste. Espresso is popular in Italian culture
and has spread all over the world. It can be prepared using an espresso machine and
is usually served without additives such as milk or sugar to enjoy its unique taste.
Espresso coffee lovers love it because it gives them a unique flavor and texture
that distinguishes it from other coffees.

COFFEE WITH CREAMY :

Coffee with cream is one of the types of coffee that is loved by many, and it
is distinguished by adding fresh cream to the prepared coffee. Cream can be
added to hot or cold coffee, and this addition increases the smoothness
and softness of the coffee. Sugar can also be added as desired to improve flavour.
Creamy coffee is a great option to add a touch of luxury and smoothness
to a cup of coffee. It can be easily prepared at home using simple
ingredients such as ground coffee, cream and sugar.

BOILED COFFEE :

Boiled coffee is one of the first types of coffee preparation, and it is characterized
by adding ground coffee directly to boiling water. Sugar or milk can be added as desired,
and this coffee is characterized by its strong and concentrated flavour.
Boiled coffee is a quick and easy coffee preparation option that can be made
anywhere, anytime.

NITRO COFFEE :

Cold brew coffee infused with Nitrogen (hence the name). Nitrogen also helps
reduce some of the bitterness and acidity of the coffee, which makes it more
palatable to even non-coffee drinkers. One of the main benefits to nitro coffee
is that it has nearly 30% more caffeine than regular drip coffee. Some scientists
also speculate that the nitrogen gas helps your body process the caffeine
quicker, so you can get your caffeine boost faster.

OUR TEA

Tea Blends and Single Origins :

Black Lavender :

A glorious bouquet of delicate French lavender paired with exquisite black
Ceylon and lapsang souchong teas, fruity black currant and sweet vanilla.
Tasting Notes: Crème Brûlée, leather, sweet berry wine.

Dandy Detox :

A naturally cleansing, caffeine-free tonic of hearty dandelion root, spicy Krishna tulsi,
mouthwatering ginger and sweet honeybush. Antioxidant-rich and delicious, this
beautifully balanced elixir gently blows all other dandelion teas away.
Tasting Notes: Roasted chicory, wildflower honey, shiso leaf.

British Brunch :

A rich blend of full-bodied Indian Assam teas paired with succulent Ceylon
Dimbula and a touch of smoky Keemun from Anhui Province in China.
The perfect breakfast tea. But sophisticated and delicious any time of day as well.
Tasting Notes: Malt, honey, mineral.

Meadow :

A caffeine-free blend of golden Egyptian chamomile flowers and mildly stimulating,
fragrant hyssop joined with smooth Cape Rooibos, rose petals and linden flowers.
You may need a sun hat. Tasting Notes: Chamomile, apple, honey.

OUR COOK

Kitchen Blends and Single Origins :

Scones :

Scones are biscuit-like pastries or quick breads that are often rolled into round shapes
and cut into quarters, then baked. Nothing like a fresh scone to say, it's going to be a
good day. Blueberry, Cinnamon, Orange-Cranberry are just a few of the savory varieties
that are sure to please.

Muffins :

Perfect For Breakfast, A Snack Or An Afternoon Pick-Me-Up Your Entire Family
Will Enjoy. Nothing says have a nice day like a muffin. Blueberry, Banana Nut,
Cinnamon, or even Chocolate -- all sure to please!!!

Cinnamon Roll :

Make your mornings, or any time of day, a little easier and a whole lot more
delicious. How? Try a cinnamon roll -- sweet baked dough filled with
cinnamon-sugar filling. Who could say no to that?
